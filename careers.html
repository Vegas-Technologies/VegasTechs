Current Openings

Network Implementation Engineer

In this role, you will install, configure, and manage enterprise networks while traveling across the country. We seek entry to mid-level Network Technicians with a current networking certification.

Key Requirements:

  • Hands-on experience with networking hardware
  • Strong troubleshooting and implementation skills
  • Willingness to travel

Data Cabler

This position offers an opportunity to work with a team of experts to install and manage structured cabling systems. BICSI certification preferred, but not required.

Key Requirements:

  • Ability to install, terminate, and test Cat5e/Cat6/Fiber cables
  • No prior experience needed – we provide training
  • Willingness to travel

Contracting Jobs

If you prefer flexibility, we offer contract-based projects in enterprise network installations, primarily data cabling and physical layer networking.

Key Requirements:

  • Experience in IT networking or structured cabling
  • Ability to manage small to large-scale projects
  • Willingness to work on-site
